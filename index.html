Wood Window Guards

Wood Window Guards are window blocks designed to prevent pets from escaping. They also prevent pets from sitting on open window sills or stools. This combination makes Wood Window Guards an effective pet safety and security product.

Wood Window Guards provide peace of mind to pet owners whenever a window must be left open and unsupervised.

A Window Barrier - Not A Window Screen

Wood Window Guards work with single and double-hung windows. If your window moves up-and-down Wood Window Guards will work.
