How To Measure

Wood Window Guards are built to any height between 6 and 18 inches and any width between 18 and 36 inches (in one inch increments).

Height
  1. Open your window to the desired height
  2. From inside the window measure the distance between sash bottom and window bottom
Width
  1. Open your window
  2. From inside the window measure the distance between window edges
  3. We recommend subtracting one inch from your width measurement to guarantee a fit
